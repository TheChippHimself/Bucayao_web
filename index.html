About Us

Bucayao IEMELIF Church, located in Bucayao, Calapan City, is a church rooted in Iglesia Evangelica Methodista En las Islas Filipinas (IEMELIF). Founded in 1926 by Rev. Fortunato M. Matibag and Pastora Florentina L. Aguila, it continues to serve as a center for worship, fellowship, and outreach, faithfully guiding its members and the surrounding community to this day.
